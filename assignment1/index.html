About The Song

The heavy metal band Metallica wrote "Nothing Else Matters" on April 20, 1992. It peaked at number 11 on the Billboard Album Rock Tracks list, number 6 on the UK singles chart, number 1 in Denmark, and in the top 10 on numerous other European charts. acknowledged as one of the most well-known and well-liked songs by Metallica

About The Band

Metallica is an American heavy metal band founded in Los Angeles in 1981 by drummer Lars Ulrich and singer/guitarist James Hetfield, they have spent the majority of its history located in San Francisco. Along with Megadeth, Anthrax, and Slayer, the band was one of the original "big four" bands of thrash metal because of its fast tempos, instrumentals, and violent musicianship.
